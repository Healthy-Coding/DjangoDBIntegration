Financial Accessibility

To help students understand the financial costs of a university we display the relative cost per income braket for each University. We also show median graduating income and debt so that students can calculate both the cost of attending and their financial status upon graduation.

Additionally we give a representation of the socio-economic diversity of a University by showing the percentage of students who are recipients of Pell grants.

Diversity Accountability

To show students the diversity of a university we compare metrics from College Board, College Data, and the National Center for Education Statistics. Additionally we display US Census state data so that students can understand how diverse the university is in comparison to its location

Resources

In the socially connected world that we live in, news stories emerge that students do not know how to respond to. To help students understand these sitatuations and formulate strong responce arguements the University Accountability Project connects students with articles and resources needed to create strong responces. In addition we allow students to submit the stories they are concerned with in order to help them provide strong and thoughtful repsonces.
